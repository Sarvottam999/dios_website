About us

About DIOS

The Neighbourhood Bistro

DIOS is more than just a restaurant – it's an experience. Located in the heart of Model Town, Panipat, we bring together exquisite delicacies and expertly crafted cocktails across two elegant floors. Our passion for fine dining and exceptional hospitality creates the perfect setting for celebrations, intimate dinners, and memorable nights out. From Unbothered Saturday to Fakira Friday and Divas Night, every visit to DIOS promises an extraordinary culinary journey.

Our Team

Meet Our Culinary Experts

At DIOS, our culinary team brings together passion, creativity, and years of expertise to craft unforgettable dining experiences. From exquisite Italian delicacies to innovative fusion dishes, our chefs pour their hearts into every plate, ensuring each dish tells a story of flavor and excellence.

Our mixologists are equally talented, creating craft cocktails that perfectly complement our menu. Whether it's a special event night or an intimate dinner, our team's dedication to quality and innovation shines through in every detail, making DIOS the premier dining destination in Panipat.
